General Meetings!

Our club hosts bi-weekly general meetings that introduce club members to important cybersecurity concepts and techniques! Each meeting can range from receiving tutorials to participating in live challenges. Anyone is welcome and no experience is required! After all, it doesn't hurt to gain some cybersecurity knowledge in a society highly driven by technology ;)

Get involved!

CyberCatamounts offers the opportunity for people of non-technical to highly technical backgrounds to apply to committees if they want to be more involved in the club! The committees are CCWebDev, CCContentCreators, and CCOutreach. Find out about the roles of each committee under the 'Join us!' tab.
